Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about how the Gym Tracker works? We’ve got you covered.

1. What is the Gym Tracker?

Gym Tracker is a fitness tracking system that helps you monitor workouts, manage progress, and visualize your goals with analytics and dashboards.

2. Can I use it on mobile devices?

Currently, you can access the web version on any device browser. A mobile app version is in development and coming soon for Android and iOS.

3. Do I need to create an account?

For now, no sign-up is required for the demo. You can freely explore the features. Account registration will be added in the full release version.

4. Is my progress data saved?

Yes! All your progress is stored locally on your browser. When cloud sync is introduced, your data will also be backed up securely online.
